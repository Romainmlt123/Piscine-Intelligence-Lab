# English Vocabulary and Expressions

## Greetings and Introductions
- Hello / Hi / Hey
- Good morning / Good afternoon / Good evening
- How are you? / How's it going?
- Nice to meet you. / Pleased to meet you.
- My name is... / I'm...

## Polite Expressions
- Please / Thank you / You're welcome
- Excuse me / I'm sorry / Pardon
- Could you please...? / Would you mind...?
- I would like... / May I have...?

## Common Idioms
- **Break a leg** - Good luck
- **Piece of cake** - Very easy
- **Under the weather** - Feeling sick
- **Hit the books** - To study
- **Cost an arm and a leg** - Very expensive
- **Once in a blue moon** - Very rarely
- **Bite the bullet** - To do something difficult

## Connectors and Linking Words
**Addition:** and, also, moreover, furthermore, in addition
**Contrast:** but, however, although, nevertheless, on the other hand
**Cause:** because, since, as, due to
**Result:** so, therefore, consequently, as a result
**Example:** for example, for instance, such as

## Question Formation
**Yes/No questions:** Do/Does/Did + subject + verb?
- Do you speak English?
- Does she work here?

**WH-questions:** WH-word + auxiliary + subject + verb?
- Where do you live?
- What time does the train leave?
- Why did you call?
